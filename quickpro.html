OUR STORY

About QuickPro Waterproof

Founded in 2010, QuickPro Waterproof has grown from a small local contractor to one of Pretoria's most trusted waterproofing and construction companies. Our journey has been built on a foundation of integrity, quality workmanship, and customer satisfaction.

What started as a specialized waterproofing service has expanded to offer comprehensive construction solutions, thanks to our team's dedication and our clients' trust. We've successfully completed hundreds of projects, from residential repairs to large commercial constructions.
