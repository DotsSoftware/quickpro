About QuickPro Waterproof

Founded in 2010, QuickPro Waterproof has established itself as a trusted partner for residential projects in Pretoria. While our company is relatively new, our team brings over 10 years of individual experience in the construction and waterproofing industry.

We specialize in residential construction and waterproofing services, focusing on quality workmanship and customer satisfaction. Our commitment to using only the highest quality materials and employing skilled professionals has helped us build a strong reputation in the community.

From small residential repairs to complete home constructions, we approach every job with dedication and attention to detail. Your satisfaction is our guarantee.
